Amenti: "Great game for a one time playtrough"

Name: Amenti

Ganre: Horror


Amenti was a nice short game to play trough, I had lots of fun with the jumpscares and the story which altough there could have been done more with it, was fun.

The notes you found along the way where whity and the exploration of the tomb was dope.

Aside from a few, in my opinion, unnecessary things like the pilar jumping part and the 'you're dead' sound effect/art, for the 8 euro's I payed this was well worth it.

Watch the video for a more detailed explaination on my toughts of it, but overal this game, for the money you pay for it gets a 7.5 out of 10!
